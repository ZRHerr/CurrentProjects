How it works

Easy to use, Easy to understand, Easy to save.

Preventative Maintenance has never been easier

Avoid the hassle of driving your car or having it towed to a service center. Time is valuable and so are our vehicles, Which is why we make the entire process easy for you as the customer or you as the business owner. Using our website or our mobile apps, you can access all local companies that partner with us and their availabilities. We keep the price fair and transparent, enable future scheduling with reminders, offer payment options through the application, accessible service history and most importantly live updates and tracking features. Check one more task off your list and use that time to focus on the more important things in life.

What we need
What you think or know is wrong (e.g.. "my car is shaking" or "I need an oil change"
The year, Make, Model of your vehicle will be accessible to our service providers through the registration process.

Confirmation
Before any commitment has been made you will have the ability to view the price details and the company that selected your job.
Reviews, Websites, all relevant information will be accessible to view through the application.
A mutual confirmation between customer and business will be needed before the service can begin.

Your Appointment
Whether you scheduled for a future date or same day service you will get text and email notifications from start to finish of that day.
Live updates with the drivers infomation and location are easily accessible.
You can speak to your mechanic directly through the app via our in app messaging system.

Appointment Details
You as the client have the option to not only select your preferred service provider, but set time requirements when filling out our request details form.
Misunderstandings or Unhappy service from either parties, Our 24 hour customer service is ready to address any concerns.
A full report of service details will be accessible and saved to your profile for future providers to see or to file away as you see fit
